ABOUT US

RideSafe is a digital platform designed to enhance the safety of ride-sharing services. By requiring all drivers and their vehicles to register, RideSafe creates a transparent database that can be accessed by passengers. This enables users to verify the identity of their driver and vehicle details through a unique five-digit code. By promoting transparency and accountability, RideSafe aims to reduce the risk of incidents and provide peace of mind to both drivers and passengers.
